About The Band

The Black Flames is a hardcore metal band known for their intense sound and explosive performances. Formed in 2010, the band has toured the world, delivering electrifying music that captivates audiences everywhere.

Members: John Doe (Vocals), Mike Smith (Guitar), Alex White (Bass), Dave Black (Drums).
